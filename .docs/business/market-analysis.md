By bridging India's 1.4B Aadhaar-verified citizens with global blockchain infrastructure, we unlock unprecedented opportunities in cross-border verification, financial inclusion, and self-sovereign identity.

## Competitive Landscape

### Traditional Identity Providers

#### Strengths
- **Established Trust**: Years of market presence
- **Regulatory Compliance**: Deep compliance expertise
- **Enterprise Relationships**: Existing customer base
- **Infrastructure**: Mature systems and processes

#### Weaknesses
- **Centralized Risk**: Single points of failure
- **High Costs**: ₹500-2,000 per verification
- **Limited Portability**: Vendor lock-in
- **Slow Innovation**: Legacy system constraints

### Blockchain Identity Solutions

#### Current Players
1. **Civic (CVC)**
   - Focus: DeFi KYC and Web3 verification
   - Limitation: No government-grade verification
   - Market Cap: ~$50M

2. **SelfKey (KEY)**
   - Focus: Self-sovereign identity for crypto
   - Limitation: Limited real-world adoption
   - Market Cap: ~$15M

3. **Microsoft ION**
   - Focus: Enterprise decentralized identity
   - Limitation: No consumer focus, complex implementation

#### Market Gaps
- **No Government Integration**: Existing solutions lack official verification
- **Limited India Focus**: No solutions built for Indian infrastructure
- **Poor UX**: Crypto-first design alienates mainstream users
- **High Friction**: Complex onboarding processes
